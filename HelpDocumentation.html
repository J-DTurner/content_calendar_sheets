Content Calendar Help & Documentation

About the Content Calendar

This Google Sheets content calendar is designed to help you organize and manage your social media content strategy using week numbers as the primary organization method.

Key Features

  • Organization by Week Number: Content is organized by ISO week numbers for easy planning.
  • Status Tracking: Visual status tracking with color coding.
  • Multiple Channels: Support for Twitter, YouTube, and Telegram.
  • Google Drive Integration: Link directly to assets in Google Drive.
  • Email Notifications: Automated notifications for status changes and deadlines.
  • Calendar Integration: Sync with Google Calendar for scheduling.
  • Content Templates: Pre-defined templates for different content types.
  • Dashboard: Visual overview of your content strategy.
  • Search & Filter: Advanced search and filtering capabilities.

Working with Week Numbers

This calendar uses ISO week numbers (where week 1 is the first week with a Thursday in the new year). Week numbers provide a consistent way to plan content across months and quarters.

Week numbers should auto-generate when you enter a date. Use "Content App > âœ¨ Show App Panel > Generate Week Numbers" to fill any missing ones.
